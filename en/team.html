Employee Database

The employees of the Mari 51 Secret Alien Base are a group of highly skilled government officials who are under the guise of CEGEP-aged students. To maintain their cover, they make up the Marianopolis Robotics Team. This allows them to have multiple meetups throughout the year without any external sources becoming suspicious of their actual alien-related activities.

Under the leadership of Janvi and Lefty, as well as the mentorship of Dominique Paradis, the Marianopolis Robotics Team is working on their performance at the FLIP 2020 competition hosted by CRC Robotics. The members of the Team each work on various sub-elements of the competition based on their skills and interests.
