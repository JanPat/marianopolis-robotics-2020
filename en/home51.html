Welcome to Mari 51

The Mari 51 Secret Alien Base is located at Marianopolis College and is run by undercover employees disguised as members of the Marianopolis Robotics Team. The Base holds many specialized extraterrestrial laboratories where scientists run experiments on different alien species and artefacts found throughout the years.

Recently, there has been growing unrest within the general public about the existence of aliens. Naturally, some are starting to become suspicious of the operations run at Mari 51. In the past months, several groups of self-proclaimed extraterrestrial truth-seekers have been planning a raid of the Mari 51 facilities to prove the existence of alien life. Luckily, they have posted all of their plans online. On this website, you can access information about the operations at the Mari 51 Secret Alien Base as well as this new security threat on the Base posed by these raiders.

If any non-Mari 51 employee passes by you when you have this website open, click the Marianopolis Robotics logo in your top right corner to escape.

Practice your Decryption Skills


The Caesar Cipher is a cipher in which each letter in the alphabet is shifted by a number, called a key. Your task is to figure out the hidden message by finding the key used to encrypt the original text.

Ciphering is often used to encode and hide messages between Mari 51 and other secret government organizations. It is one of the reasons why the public has been unaware of the operations at the Base for so long.
